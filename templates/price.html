Pricing, Properties and Progress of AirBnB




This map follows from our previous location ratings by neighbourhood map. Now it is obvious that the highly rated location would also tend to be costly (demand vs supply). This graph would be a good opportunity to put our previous results to test.Again downtown Asheville is the clear winner when it comes to high rents followed by Biltmore.



It is interesting to note that there has been a decline in number of 'new listings' in the recent years! Hosting trends were at peak around 2015, when we saw most number of new listings. Given that the total number of AirBnBs in and around the area are 2,387.It does look that the 'Bed & Breakfast' market has reached a saturation.
