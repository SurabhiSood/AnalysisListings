Exploratory Data Analysis and Visualization of Airbnb Dataset





Hola! bienvido a mi applicacion. My work presents exploratory data analysis for Airbnbs @Asheville through, map visualizations, interactive plots, animations and lots of other interesting javascript libraries.

My Quest with this dataset was to analyze:

  • How do prices of listings vary by location?
  • What are the different types of properties in Asheville? Do they vary by neighborhood?
  • What localities in Asheville are rated highly by guests?
  • What makes a host Super host?

Data was sourced from http://insideairbnb.com/get-the-data.html. Brief Description of the data i worked on

  • Detailed listings - data showing 96 atttributes for each of the listings. Some of the attributes used in the analysis are price (continuous), longitude (continuous), latitude (continuous), listing_type (categorical), is_superhost (categorical), neighbourhood (categorical), ratings (continuous) among others.

The data shows that there are:

  • There are 2,387 unique listings in Asheville. First host was listed on October 23, 2009.
  • Price per night ranges from $10 to $250. The most expensive property is Southern Gem located at downtown Asheville
  • You can rent a whole Castle too!
